# Kickstarter

Kickstarter helps artists, musicians, filmmakers, 
designers, and other creators find the resources 
and support they need to make their ideas a reality. 
To date, tens of thousands of creative projects — 
big and small — have come to life with the support 
of the Kickstarter community.
